Interests

Research interests:

  • Catalysis
  • Automation, 3D printing, robotics and AI-driven digital synthesis
  • Synthetic organic and inorganic chemistry
  • Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs)
  • Design and synthesis of novel organometallic compounds
  • Evaluation of coordination compounds using X-ray crystallography and spectroscopic methods
    • My research spans the areas of organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry, and focuses on the design and fabrication of functional nanomaterials that show novel magnetic, optical, and electronic phenomena. Potential applications range from catalysis, sensors and the capture of greenhouse gases, etc. The significant key aspect is understanding the functional relationships between structural features of solid-state materials, solutions, and their physical properties.
